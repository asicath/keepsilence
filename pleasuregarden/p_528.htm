The Rhodostaurotic and Heaven-Carrying Atlas

Atlas carries on his shoulders the burden of the whole world.
With you mind carry, without complaint, heaven and the earth.

Then you will speedily arrive at the foundation of all things,
and soon the stone from the earth will be known to you.

Be always wise and prudent, without cunning towards others.
Be ever plain and simple, betray nobody, and you will be happy.
