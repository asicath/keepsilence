The Other Key of Basilius

When the garment is taken off. Then the sun appears.
Dianna no longer wears her garment, so that marriage becomes more desirable.

From two noble fencers the bride receives delicious water,
so that she can bathe her own body for her groom.

The fighters may well fight. But if the fight comes to an end on both sides,
the fighters bring out of the fight treasures and great booty.
