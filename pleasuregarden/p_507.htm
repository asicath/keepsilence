The Nourishing

Here is our lovely garden, for our fruit rests here.
Mercurius appears as if he is ready for a quick flight.

You, most beautiful king, with your might seize quickly this booty
because for your noble stomach no other booty will be more appropriate.

Utilize the seized so that the limbs do not grow flabby,
and from ravenous hunger gradually enfeeble.
