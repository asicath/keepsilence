Raymund Lully, a Spaniard

Raymund confesses openly that he finally came
to the perfection of the chemical art through the favors of his teacher Arnold.

In reason he was Proteus, in philosophy Policletus,
in art he equalled Daedalus, he was a great honor to the Spanish people.

He says: A man produces the beautiful body of a child
if he takes to his side a woman, as is usually the case.
