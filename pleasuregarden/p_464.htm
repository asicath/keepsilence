Vegetable Things
That is, what grows upon the earth

Like a garden greens beautifully and bears many fine herbs,
so also this our garden bears many forms and kinds.

Here stand the beautiful hyacinth, also grapevines,
wheat and poppies, and red roses.

Golden apples and mulberry trees can here be found;
Myrtle, laurel, olive trees, liquid-amber, and the prettiest saffron.
