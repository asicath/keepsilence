Mercurius or Quicksilver

In Mercurius is at all times what the wise ones seek:
He represents the highest treasures of the entire world.

The great flame of the fire-eating father does not damage Mercurius.
He always flies away from the warm hearth.

Yet if you can keep him there, stiff and fine,
then you will be considered blissful here on earth by his child.
