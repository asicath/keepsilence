The Green Lion

What does the lion with its star signify? And what does the sun that likes to be
held tight in the lion's paw? Tell me, Mufa, as best you can!

This our couragous hero is the green lion
in whose innermost abdomen the red star also lies hidden.

The green lion is extremely agile and blows forth the sun's colors
so that the reflection may be seen in the plain.
