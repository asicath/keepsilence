Three Prossessors of the Philosopher's Stone

Here you see, dear reader, three artists of the highest art,
Who through their zeal have succeeded in obtaining the stone, so highly esteemed.

The known Cremerus stands in the center, Nortonus to the left,
At the right Basilius; Their praise cannot be passed in silence.

Read zealously their works, then also use the volcanic tools,
if you want to pluck the golden apples in the land of the Hesperides.
