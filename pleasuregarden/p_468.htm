The Four Effects, Disintigration, Lavation, Composition, and Stabtility

Four beautiful sisters of fine forms and equal fortune are portrayed to you.
They show you, in a pleasent way, what your work is like.

The first one tells you to disperse any part resulting from disintigration.
The other teaches you how you lave together such excellenct materials.

The third advises you to put together seperate parts;
And the fourth, how to stabilize the stone in the fire.
