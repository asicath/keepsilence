Animal Things
That is, all that is animal and has life, or the living creatures

Over hill and vale many animals walk and run.
The air carries many birds; The water is full of fish.

But more important to the artists is the ram, bull, lion, and bear,
the mule, and the wild pig, the lynx, wolf, and the dog.

Look at the eagle, phoenix, and peacock, the raven and the swan;
Do not forget the whale, adders, otters, and the bees.
