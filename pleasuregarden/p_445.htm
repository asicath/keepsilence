The Eleventh Key of Basilius

Orpheus took Euridice, or the brother took the sister,
ashis wife so that the blood from both bodies may become pure.

Thus, unite the sweat of father and mother, when it is still warm, industriously.
Let it enter into and seal it up at once in the globe of the white Master.

Then, with pleasure, you will see the generous lion standing.
It will see so many of its body's fruits that they cannot be counted.
