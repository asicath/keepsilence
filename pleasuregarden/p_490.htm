The Elixir or an Oily Juice in Flux

He who desires to know what the elixir is, and what it is
that renders all metals amenable to his work:

Firstly, must to this end seek such a medicament
that flows everywhere before the quick flight of mercury.

Unite to this the sulphur of the king or queen;
And then the one bird betrays the other that flies away.
