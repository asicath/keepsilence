The Rubefaction

The king is resurrected from the fetter of death and brings new pleasure.
He regales with his gifts those who before were sad and depressed.

He is fond of his colleagues and crowns them with clear gold.
The tender limbs this time he clothes with genuine purple.

Here the true Azoth is free, and also the medicine of the wise,
which restore with their powerful things sick limbs to health.
