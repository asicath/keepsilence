The Philosophic Ore of the Wise or Copper

Our living ore, pure and clear, is the gold of the wise men.
Because with its rays it illuminates all things.

It be then that you add to it its own "Duenech;"
Otherwise you will not have for such marriage the desired wedding bed.

If then the two lions come together, and also their hearts,
from their mouths will flow, a golden juice.
