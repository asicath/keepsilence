The First Key of Basilius Valentinus

The crown of the king shall be of gold, beautiful, pure and clear.
A chaste bride is led to her groom; that is as it ought to be.

Then give to eat to the king the grey wolf that wants to eat continuously.
This do three times with a strong fire; burn the wolf entirely.

Then the king will come forth; without a blemish, clean, he will stand before you.
He can decorate you with his blood, and renovate you.
